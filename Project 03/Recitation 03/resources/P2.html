▣ What is a compound word?
► It is a word that is formed by combining two words together.

        Here are some examples of compound words:
        cannot
        crosswalk
        airport
        butterfly

        As you can see, in the first example, the word "cannot" is created by combining the words "can" and "not".
        Similar to the second example, "crosswalk" is formed by combining "cross" and "walk".
